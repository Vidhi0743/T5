The Da Vinci Mystery - Clue 4

Uncover the secret ingredients of an Italian dish loved by millions.

Clue 4: "I am the king of Italian cuisine, with four parts mixed in perfect harmony. My crust, my sauce, my cheese, and my topping, each unique, yet together they are unstoppable. I am baked in an oven, but my name comes from the city where a royal assembly first fell in love with me."

Think of an iconic Italian dish with a long history, often associated with a royal or celebratory occasion. The answer lies in both the ingredients and the city that made it famous.

Correct! The answer is "Pizza Margherita," named after Queen Margherita of Savoy, and created in Naples. Now you can proceed to the next clue.
